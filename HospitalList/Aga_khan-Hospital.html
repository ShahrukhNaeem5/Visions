    About The Aga Khan University Hospital

    The Aga Khan University Hospital in Karachi, established in 1985. Their vision is to provide premier, tertiary, referral health care facilities to the people of Pakistan.

    Timing

    The Aga Khan University Hospital offers 24/7 hours of emergency services.

    Consultation Fee

    The average consultation fee at The Aga Khan University Hospital is 1500- 5000 RS. .

    The Aga Khan University Hospital services

    The Aga Khan University Hospital based on 560 beds, 122 private and 117 semi-private air-conditioned rooms, 251 General Ward beds, and 52 special care beds are available in ICU, CCU, and NICU. The hospital is equipped to diagnose and treat medical including cardiac, surgical, obstetrics and gynecology, pediatrics, and psychiatry patients. The hospital has 17 main operating theatres; there are 4 operating theatres in Surgical Day Care and 2 in Obs/Gyn.

    Services Available at The Aga Khan University Hospital are given below:

    • Day Care Surgeries Pharmacy
